I'm Usman Ibrahim, a multi-disciplinary technologist based in Funtua, Nigeria, with over 10 years of hands-on experience spanning software development, graphic design, and technology management. I believe technology should solve real problems for real people — and that's what drives everything I build.

I currently serve as Hub Manager at Afraah Tech Hub, where I oversee programs, nurture emerging talent, and drive innovation across the community. I also lead as Head of Management Information Systems at Delight Schools, where I apply technology to strengthen institutional operations.

My work ranges from desktop exam-prep software and Android games to full hub management — all united by a commitment to quality, discipline, and impact.
